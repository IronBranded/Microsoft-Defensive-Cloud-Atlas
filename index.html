Toggle your active licenses in the panel above. Select multiple licenses across any scope — M365, Defender, Sentinel, Azure/Entra, and Purview. ⚠ CRITICAL RETENTION GAPS FOR DFIR
▸ No Entra P1? Sign-in logs visible for only 7 days. After that, authentication history is gone.
▸ No Advanced Audit (E5)? MailItemsAccessed is not logged at all. Cannot determine which emails were read.
▸ No Diagnostic Settings? Key Vault, Storage, SQL, and Firewall logs evaporate immediately.
▸ MDE 30-day hard limit: Endpoint telemetry purged after 30 days. Ingest to Sentinel for longer windows.
▸ Exchange Message Trace hard cap: Cannot be extended beyond 90 days regardless of license.
▸ Purview IRM: Insider Risk alerts only visible if IRM license is active — retrospective alerting is not possible.
